This House Would...

This is a POLICY MOTION, based on whether or not we should implement/enact a certain policy.

Usually, the one implementing the policy is the government unless stated otherwise. Assumptions are made about feasibility and effectiveness.

This House Believes that X should...

This is a POLICY MOTION, whether or not X should do Y or a certain action.

Government/Proposition teams can propose how Y would be done, based on reasonable assumptions. Debated from a neutral perspective.

This House Believes That...

This is a VALUE JUDGEMENT MOTION, whether or not a statement is true.

It is not about government action, but about TRUE/FALSE statements.
