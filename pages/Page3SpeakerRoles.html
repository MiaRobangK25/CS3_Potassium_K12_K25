Speaker Roles

In debate, there are two main formats, Asian & British Parliamentary

For AP ( Asian Parliamentary 3v3 )…

Think of the PROCESS of making PANCAKES. Each side, GOVERNMENT AND OPPOSITION, needs to make the BEST set of pancakes.

Prime Minister & Leader of Opposition

JOB: During preparation time, all of the ingredients (ideas) are mixed and cooked into pancakes!

The pancakes by the first speaker must be shown to be cooked, baked, and are indeed pancakes to the judge, for all the other toppings and add-ons!

They set up the Debate, the basis for the rest of the round. Arguments/main points must be clear.

Deputy Prime Minister & Deputy Leader of Opp.

JOB: Assist with undercooked pancakes, respond to other team, rebuild, and extend arguments.

Government and Opposition Whip

JOB: Add special toppings, summarize the debate, respond to others, and advertise their pancakes as the best.

For BP ( British Parliamentary 2v2v2v2 )…

Each side of the GOVERNMENT/PROPOSITION and OPPOSITION is split into two. Positions are decided at random!

  • OPENING GOVERNMENT (OG)
  • CLOSING GOVERNMENT(CG)
  • OPENING OPPOSITION(OO)
  • CLOSING OPPOSITION(CO)

Think of BP as each team of 2 at OG, OO, CG, and CO competing to build the BEST, MOST IMPORTANT (yet unique) BURGER.

Prime Minister & Leader of Opposition

JOB: Build the BEST burger they can during prep, same specifics as AP format.

Deputy Prime Minister & Deputy Leader of Opposition

JOB: Build on and sell the burger from 1st Speaker, same specifics as AP format.

Member of Government / Opposition

JOB: Build a UNIQUE burger and provide extensions, rebuttals, or characterization.

Government / Opposition Whip

JOB: Similar to deputies, advertise and summarize their distinct burger.
