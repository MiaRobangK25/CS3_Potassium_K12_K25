1. The Most Popular Debate Formats

The Philippine Debate Circuit is very competitive. Debate is a sport, a sport a lot of people take seriously-- or find solace in. Yet, at the same time, debate is also very flexible! In the Philippine world of debate-- there are two popular formats. The first is A] ASIAN PARLIAMENTARY (3v3), and B] BRITISH PARLIAMENTARY (2v2v2v2).

2. Sides of a Debate, and Preparation Time

There is a side FOR the motion, and a side AGAINST the motion. These sides are referred to as PROPOSITION/GOVERNMENT and OPPOSITION.

3. Speeches and Speaker Positions

Each person/debater has 7 minutes and 15 seconds to speak, with special reply speeches depending on format.

4. Quick Overview of Motions

Motions are written in the format "THIS HOUSE" and can vary widely in topic and framing.
