Motion Analysis

This House Would...

This is a POLICY MOTION, based on whether or not we should implement/enact a certain policy.

Usually, the one implementing the policy is the government– unless stated otherwise. This is based on the assumption that on the side where the policy is enacted, it is FEASIBLE for it to be implemented; however, quality and effectiveness are not.

This House Believes that X should...

This is a POLICY MOTION, whether or not X should do Y or a certain action.

Government/Proposition teams can also propose how Y would be done, based on reasonable assumptions. Although this motion type must be debated from the perspective of a NEUTRAL OBSERVER.

This House Believes That...

This is a VALUE JUDGEMENT MOTION, whether or not a statement is true.

It is not whether or not the government should do anything about the statement; it is about whether something is TRUE/FALSE.
