Speaker Roles

In debate, there are two main formats, Asian & British Parliamentary

PM & LO

Presents & highlights framing and arguments from their respective bench.

DPM & DLO

Replies to the opponents’ arguments & extends their bench’s arguments.

The Member

Reply to the opponents’ arguments & rebuttals. Presents new arguments.

The Whip

Summarizes their side’s arguments and compares them to the opponents’.

Both formats allot 7 minutes & 15 seconds for speakers to deliver their speeches
